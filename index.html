Skills 13 active

Where they live: ~/.claude/skills/ (6 Custom BeaverMind + 7 Morningside AI)
💡 Think of skills like saved recipes -- you say "/linkedin-post" and Claude knows exactly what to do, every time.

Agents On-demand
What: One-off workers Claude launches for complex tasks. Unlike skills (reusable) or scheduled tasks (recurring), agents are spawned for a specific job and disappear when done.
Where they live: They don't have a permanent home -- Claude creates them in the moment when a task needs parallel or isolated work.
💡 Think of agents like hiring a freelancer for one job -- they do it, report back, and you move on.

MCP Connections 10 services
What: Live bridges between Claude and your external tools. Each MCP lets Claude directly read/write to a service (Gmail, Slack, Supabase, etc.) without you copy-pasting.
Active: Gmail, Calendar, Slack, Notion, Supabase, GitHub, Drive, n8n, GoHighLevel, ElevenLabs
💡 MCPs are why Claude can "check your email" or "post to Slack" -- they're the hands Claude uses to touch your tools.

Where it lives: CLAUDE.md in Ruben HQ root + ~/.claude/projects/*/memory/MEMORY.md
💡 CLAUDE.md is why Claude knows your tech stack, your clients, and your preferences without you repeating them every time.
